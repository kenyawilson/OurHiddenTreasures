Dive into what we do!

We are on a mission to prepare our youth for their future, a future that will ultimately shape and grow the booming workforce of the South Carolina Upstate. Everyone should be given the opportunity to have a sanctuary that encourages elevation, provides a caring environment, and as a result fosters their innate desire to find their Hidden Treasures.

Our Hidden Treasures vows to provide essential foundation in the form of supportive relationships, educational activities, and creative exploration. With this common goal in mind, this is our calling, our resolution, and our mission. Find out how to get involved with our active campaigns below!

Featured Campaigns

Biscuits & Coffee

Sponsor

Bimonthly event where we serve snacks and beverages to the community. Our most recent Biscuits & Coffee event served 30 personal bags. Each bag had an inspiration message on the tag to read. Inside each bag was a bottle water, 2 nutra-grain bars, a pack of tuna and one cup of fruit.

Monthly Virtual Workout

Sponsor

We encourage healthy living in the Upstate area currently with a monthly virtual workout! This workout is for everyone, and FREE for all to "attend." Our goal in offering this experience is to give everyone a reprieve from the ongoing chaos and uncertainty, to help keep their bodies keep moving as we're all "stuck" at home, and to help guide their minds and hearts back to a place of peace and calm.

Back to School Event

Sponsor

We always strive to provide as many resources as possible for our youth. Whether it's through school supplies, donating clothes, or hosting a carnival for the kids, we vow to alway uplift our youth.

Winter Coat Drive

Sponsor

A Day of giving back. We provide coats, gloves, shoes, food, and more!

Personal Finance Event

Sponsor

The need for financial education for teens and young adults has never been greater. We facilitate personal finance events around the upstate to help empower and educate our community on healthy financial habits.
