Our Mission

Our focus is to unlock our hidden treasures one mind at a time.  We will accomplish this by promoting self-love and teaching vital life skills.  Skills such as Financial Literacy, Health & Wellness, and the importance of providing free-will humanitarian services in our community.  These attributes and many more will shift our conscious mind to not only excel in our day to day humanitarian endeavors but also, lead and transfer (these) qualities in our personal and surrounding connections.

Meet Our Board

Founder

April’s commitment to others stems from her personal relationships. Some of her most precious titles include friend, daughter, sister and aunt. As an adolescent, April watched her father and mother serve in a Pastoral role in the church. They were and continue to be a model for seeing the best in others and providing an outlet of added support to those in need. As the oldest of 5 brothers, nurturing and showing love to her siblings, nieces and nephews has been a great joy. She always makes time for them and that general willingness to help others birthed the vision for Our Hidden Treasures.

What started as trips to the skating rink or manicure pampering sessions for those who may be less fortunate, has turned into an opportunity to encourage and help people realize their own potential. April’s most prized hidden treasures include compassion, strong work ethic and a servant’s heart. As the Executive Director of Our Hidden Treasures, Inc, April is dedicated to inspiring the society of Upstate South Carolina to become treasured assets in their community because everyone is called to empower someone
